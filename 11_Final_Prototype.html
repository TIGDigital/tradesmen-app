What's in this prototype

Three personas, end-of-day sign-off as the hero flow. Switch personas above; the step pills under the switcher will update to show that persona's main screens.

Tradesman flow

Jobs → tap Emma's project → see the subtle "when you head home" prompt → tap to open the EoD card → send → success preview. Or jump to the lock-screen push to see the auto-trigger.

Customer flow

Project shows live state ("Dave is on site"). The Updates tab is where Dave's EoD update lands as the hero card with photos and tomorrow's start time.

Apprentice flow

Same EoD pattern, but the update routes to Dave (the lead) for approval — never directly to Emma. Hours auto-tracked from the geofence doubles as payroll proof.

Language choices that stuck

"End your day at Emma's?" instead of "Sign off for today." "Heading home?" in the push. "Send to Emma" not "Send update". The customer's name appears everywhere so the tradesman remembers who he's writing to.
